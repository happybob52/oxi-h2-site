Harnessing Hydrogen & Electrolytes for Better Health

At Oxi we believe in evidence‑based nutrition. Our gummies combine the emerging science of molecular hydrogen with the proven benefits of essential electrolytes. In this article we explore what hydrogen and electrolytes do in the body and why they matter.

What Is Molecular Hydrogen?

Molecular hydrogen (H₂) is a tasteless, colorless gas that dissolves in water. When regular water is enriched with hydrogen gas, the result is hydrogen‑rich water. According to WebMD, scientists believe hydrogen‑rich water may offer health benefits, but more research is needed【91697072338896†L172-L201】. Early studies suggest it could reduce side effects of radiation therapy【91697072338896†L181-L201】, improve athletic performance and recovery【91697072338896†L203-L210】 and provide antioxidant and anti‑inflammatory effects that may help protect against disease【91697072338896†L214-L223】.

Research is still in its infancy. Small clinical trials have looked at hydrogen water’s impact on conditions ranging from heart health to liver function and metabolic syndrome【91697072338896†L223-L267】. While some results are promising, experts emphasize that staying hydrated in general is key【91697072338896†L172-L177】.

Why Electrolytes Matter

Electrolytes are minerals that carry an electrical charge when dissolved in water. Because an adult body is around 60 % water, nearly every cell and fluid contains electrolytes. The Cleveland Clinic notes that they help regulate chemical reactions and maintain fluid balance inside and outside cells【621768199440135†L65-L71】. Your cells also use electrolytes to conduct electrical charges, which is how muscles contract and how your nerves send signals【621768199440135†L103-L106】.

UnitedHealthcare explains that electrolytes keep the body hydrated, regulate pH levels and support muscle and nerve function【156955213346932†L36-L41】. Key electrolytes include sodium, potassium, calcium, magnesium and chloride. Each has specific roles, such as regulating fluid balance (sodium), supporting bone and tooth health (calcium) and helping muscles and nerves fire correctly (magnesium and potassium)【156955213346932†L45-L58】.
